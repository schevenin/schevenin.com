About Us

PHS • Soccer • Fundraising

The Pioneer Boys Soccer Booster Club is a charitable, non-profit, fully recognized 501(c)(3) organization. We are a group of parents, staff and other interested parties whose mission is to enrich the Pioneer High School athletic community and the community at large by providing financial support for the Pioneer High School boys soccer program. The Booster Club was established as a support to encourage parent participation and to raise funds to enhance and expand the soccer program at Pioneer High School. The Booster Club believes strongly that parent involvement during the high school years can be a key to the success of the individual student. In addition to financial support, the Booster Club recognizes that participation in high school athletics benefits everyone, and the Booster Club promotes opportunities to build and strengthen relationships among all involved: the student body, Pioneer High School staff, parents, and the community. Money will be raised through memberships and fundraising activities. Throughout the year, the Booster Club plans to organize a dinner, concessions at athletic events, and to sell specialty products. All members of the Pioneer community are invited and encouraged to become part of the Booster Club and our activities.
